Increment property values with the following shortcuts:

| Increment Value | key-stroke                             |
|:---------------:|:--------------------------------------:|
| .1              | `alt` + `up` & `alt` + `down`          |
| 1               | `up` & `down`                          |
| 10              | `shift` + `up` & `shift` + `down`      |
